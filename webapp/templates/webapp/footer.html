Telerehabilitation Application for Physical Therapists (TAPhT) is a web-based application that serves as a telemedicine platform for Physical Therapists and their patients. This app will help in the management and scheduling of appointments and consultations with the goal of enabling patients and therapists alike access to these services through the internet.
